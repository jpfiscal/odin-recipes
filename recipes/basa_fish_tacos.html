Odin's Basa Fish Tacos

Description

Try this when you're tired of just air frying fish and squeezing lemon on it to eat with some rice and veggies.

Ingredients

For the Fish:

  • 8oz of Basa Fillet
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Corn Flour
  • 0.5 tsp Paprika
  • 0.5 tsp Chili Powder
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 0.25 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 0.25 tsp Onion Powder
  • 0.5 tsp Salt
  • 4 10" tortillas (flour or corn)

For the Coleslaw:

  • 0.5 lbs of Finely Chopped Cabbage
  • 0.25 Cup Mayo
  • 1 tbsp Liquid Honey
  • 1 tbsp White Vinegar
  • 0.25 tsp Salt
  • 0.25 tsp Pepper
  • 0.25 tsp Paprika

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp Mayo
  • 1 Lemon/lime Juice
  • 1 tsp Finely Grated Lime/Lemon Zest
  • 0.25 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Crushed/Finely Minced

Steps

Fish

  1. Cut the Basa fillet into 4 equal (as possible) pieces
  2. Mix the dry ingredients from the "Fish" section together in a shallow bowl
  3. Coat the Basa pieces in olive oil and roll them into the shallow bowl of dry mixture. Make sure to fully coat the entire fillet.
  4. Carefully place the coated Basa pieces into the air fryer
  5. Set the temperature of the air fryer to 390 degrees (F) and set the timer for 10 minutes and start cooking the fish
  6. After 10 minutes, open the air fryer and flip the pieces carefully as they are brittle and cook for another 3~5 minutes.

Coleslaw

  1. Chop the cabbage into thin slices
  2. In a separate bowl, mix in the Mayo, Honey, Vinegar, Salt, Pepper and Paprika
  3. Mix in the cabbage and mix well
  4. Cover and set aside in the fridge for now

Sauce

  1. Mince or crush the garlic clove
  2. Mix the minced/crushed garlic in a bolw with the rest of the ingredients in the "Sauce" section

Combine

  • Heat up a pan over medium heat
  • Place each tortilla on the pan for 10 seconds per side
  • Load each tortilla with the air fried basa, coleslaw and sauce
